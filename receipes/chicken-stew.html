Chicken Stew

Description

chicken stew is effortless to make and requires only one pot! The recipe combines succulent chicken pieces with flavorsome vegetables, resulting in a wholesome and satisfying meal. To complete this perfect dish for chilly weather, pair it with a crispy loaf of bread.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • potatoes
  • Chicken stock
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Bay leaf
  • Flour
  • Parsley/li>

Step by step

  1. Step 1: Over high heat, cook chicken until brown on all sides. Transfer to a plate.
  2. Step 2: carrots and onions. Cook until the onions begin to soften. Add garlic and cook until fragrant. Deglaze the dutch oven with some of the chicken stock.
  3. Step 3: Make a slurry by whisking flour with 1/2 cup broth in a small bowl. Add to the dutch oven. Stir in chicken, remaining broth, and bay leaf. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 25 minutes over medium-low heat.
  4. Step 4: Add potatoes and cook until potatoes are tender. Stir in parsley and discard bay leaf before serving.
